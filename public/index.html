Problem Statement

                
Traditional resume screening is time-consuming, inconsistent, and misses hidden talent. HR teams spend 23 hours per week manually reviewing resumes.

            


            

                

                    
                

                
Our Solution

                
ResumeAI uses advanced NLP to extract, analyze, and visualize resume data instantly. We transform hours of work into seconds with AI precision.

            


            

                

                    
                

                
How It Works

                
Upload → NLP Parsing → Data Extraction → AI Analysis → Visual Dashboard. Our system processes 50+ data points per resume.

            


            

                

                    
                

                
Tech Stack

                
Node.js + Express backend, Vanilla JS frontend, Advanced NLP algorithms, Canvas-based visualizations, Real-time processing.
